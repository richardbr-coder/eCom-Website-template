Licensing

We can take you through a 3 week train program to get you fully licensed and teach you what to do and what not to do. If your UAV is over 250g transport Canada requires you to complete a basic or advanced license. This exam is not free and will not be common knowlegde. A strong knowledge is frequency generation and interferace knowledge is needed as UAV pilots have to contend with millions of broadband, AM, FM, 5Ghz, 2.5Ghz and 5.8Ghz signals
