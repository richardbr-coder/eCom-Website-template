What we consider

When it comes to the custom design of a UAV there is an extensive list of components to consider. Your needs will be the foundation of the build. Once the intent behind the build is determined we can begin the planning and building phase.

The cost of a custom build can range anywhere from $100.00CAD to $30,000.00CAD and no two builds will be the same. See the list below for what is typically included in a build but please keep in mind that this is a starting point and your build may be vastly different.

Flight Controller

Flight Controllers are the life blood of any UAV. They are where all the components come together to make a stable UAV with all the hardware & software working together

Frame

Frames is what will hold all of the hardware components. Depending on your application the frame shape and size can vary greatly

ESCs

An ESC is converts the DC power coming from the LIPO battery into a 3 phase signal to be used by the motors commutator or in a brushless situation the motor windings

Motors

The motors used is a UAV will change in size depending on the task trying to be achived. a 1000KVA motor will have more torque than a motor with a 3500KVA winding rating. This is important when eciding if you want speed, lifting power or a balance

GPS

Just about all UAVs have some form of GPS. This is a crucial component as without it the UAV cannot understand where it is too globally and ca not return to home or maintain flight without drifting around. GPS units work with posistioning sensors to maintain drift free flight and return to home features in the case signal is lost to the transmitter

FPV

FPV is your eyes. Its how you see what the UAV sees. It includes a heads up display with telemetry information and a DVR if recording is needed. These can be mounted on 3-axis gimbals or be hard mounted to make room for a more professional axis mounted mirrorless camera

Transmitter

The transmitter is how to will interact with the UAV. Unlike manufactured UAVs, where you are simiply directing the it, with custom builds you are a pilot and will require training and licensing to operate it with confidence

Bluetooth

Bluetooth is not used for interacting with the UAV during flight as the range is much to short. The main purpose of this is to allow for the change of flight characteristics while on the grround and remove the need to bring laptops to the feild

Retracts

In most situations retracts are not needed but for people who need a professional videography solution they are a must

Commercial peripherals

The possiblities for commerical applactions is endless with parcel delivery and crop irrigation being some of the most demanded customizations

Ground Station

Grounds stations are all in one units that can be fitted with what ever you'd like. Bigger screens and on board controls is always a winner for any pilot

Lipo Power

LIPO batteries is the life blood of a UAV. Its how we power everything, to help with system stablity and relaiblity we wire these in parrel for longer run times and in series to provide more cells, ie more torque to the windings giving you greate speed and lifting capabilities
